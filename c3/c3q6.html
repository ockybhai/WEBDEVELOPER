MCA Full Form is Master of Computer Application. MCA is a postgraduate course that trains students in the various aspects of computer programs, application software, computer architecture, operating systems and many more. The MCA Course duration is 2 years. See Also: Full forms of MCA

The MCA admission will be done on the basis of merit or entrance examination

Apart from full-time MCA programs, there are other types of MCA in India. Different types of MCA include MCA Online and Distance MCA. Colleges like Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham or Lovely Professional University provide MCA courses in an Online format. Chandigarh University or IGNOU are some of the colleges that provide MCA courses in distance format
